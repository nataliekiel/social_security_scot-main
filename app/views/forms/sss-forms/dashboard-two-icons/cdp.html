Child Disability Payment


If you are eligible, you can apply for Child Disability Payment with Special Rules for Terminal Illness.

Warning
Special Rules for Terminal Illness: A person is considered to be terminally ill if they have a progressive disease from which death may reasonably be expected. The individual’s registered medical practitioner or registered nurse must make the clinical diagnosis. You can find more information on Terminal illness (opens in a new tab) online.

If this does not apply, you can continue to apply through the standard Child Disability Payment application.
