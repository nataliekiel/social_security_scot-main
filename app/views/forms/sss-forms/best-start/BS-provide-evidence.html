You will need to provide more evidence to get Best Start Grant and Best Start Foods

You can still apply for Best Start Grant and Best Start Foods if you do not get at least one of the benefits that show you look after the child.

To show you're the main person looking after a child, they should be named on either your or your partner's claim for:

  • Child Benefit
  • Child Tax Credit
  • Universal Credit
  • Pension Credit

If the child is not named on any of these benefits, you'll need to have a legal order that shows that you look after the them.

For Best Start Grant and Best Start Foods you'll need one of these:

  • a Kinship Care Order
  • a Compulsory Supervision Order
  • a Residence Order
  • a Permanence Order
  • a Parental Order
  • an Adoption Order

Once you've submitted your application you'll be directed to a page that tells you how to send this.
